SO, WHO AM I?

My Analytical Mindset

Operational problems rarely start as major failures. They begin as small issues, such as failed payments, repeated service problems, or slow workflows, that quietly drain time, revenue, and focus.

That is where my work starts.

Using customer and operational data, I investigate the patterns behind these issues and turn recurring problems into practical improvements. I have worked across operational and fintech environments, including at M-KOPA, where I analyzed customer behavior, repayment patterns, and service performance in pay-as-you-go systems.

My approach is shaped by a background in accounting and operations. I look at how the business actually runs: how money flows, where resources are lost, and how decisions in one area affect results in another.

The result is clear priorities teams can act on, improving efficiency, reducing repeated issues, and supporting better decisions.
